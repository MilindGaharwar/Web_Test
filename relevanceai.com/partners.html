Frequently Asked Questions

What is a partner?

A partner is either a certified Relevance AI builder or an affiliate.

How to become certified?

To become a certified agent builder, you need to complete our training program. This program equips you with the skills to effectively build and onboard AI agents. Once completed, you will receive certification and support from our team.

What are the benefits?

Partners gain access to exclusive resources, training, and support from Relevance AI. This collaboration enhances your service offerings and helps you stand out in the market. Additionally, you can tap into our growing network of clients.

Is there a fee?

There is no upfront fee to become a partner.

How do I apply?

You can apply to become a partner through our online application form. Once submitted, our team will review your application and reach out to discuss next steps. We look forward to collaborating with you!
