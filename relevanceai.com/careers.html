A little bit about us

Relevance AI is a team based in Sydney and San Francisco that believes that by 2025, every company will have a team of AI employees, their AI Workforce. This will enable teams to be limited by only their ideas, not their size.

Our Core Values

Truth-seeking

We pursue truth relentlessly, even when uncomfortable. From self-assessment to customer feedback, we face reality head-on. We deliver honest insights with kindness and separate emotions from facts.

Be empathetic

We actively understand our teammates' challenges to offer timely support and treat each other with compassion.

Put the customer first

We solve customer problems urgently and proactively. We'll go to any length to deliver value and exceed expectations, even if it doesn't scale. Swift communication is our norm.

Have two gears

We shift seamlessly between rapid action and careful deliberation, matching our pace to each challenge.

Build memories

We’re part of something special and we know when we look back, we’ll cherish the memories we create. We seek to create memories with those around us, sometimes funny, sometimes happy and sometimes sad. We prioritise the sentimental.
